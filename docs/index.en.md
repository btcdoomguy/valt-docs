## What is Valt? ğŸ’¡

Valt is a free and open-source desktop application that lets you manage your personal finances with a unique perspective: **see all your wealth in Bitcoin terms**.

Unlike traditional apps that only show values in fiat currency, Valt automatically converts all your transactions to satoshis (the smallest unit of Bitcoin), allowing you to understand the true opportunity cost of your financial decisions.
